Services

I'm a licensed professional counselor with over 10 years of experience helping children, adolescents, and adults work through some of life's challenging situations.  I strive  to help in a variety of areas:  depression, anxiety, self-harm, grief, marital issues, communication, pre-marital counseling, life coaching, and post-secondary readiness. 

Types of Therapies

Each client and each situation is unique; therefore, it is best to develop a therapeutic plan that fits the client and the presenting issue.  Some of these therapies might include: cognitive therapy, reality based therapy,  and solution-based therapy.  A variety of resources are utilized to develop a comprehensive treatment plan.
